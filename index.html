Photo gallery of regions from around the world

The world is full of different places you can visit. Below are the regions from around the world. Click on each image to zoom in, get an idea if this could be a place you would like to visit.

Map of Rome

Rome is a very popular holiday destination at the moment. Have a look at the map of Rome, our travel team have pin pointed a few holiday tourist attractions, accomadation and resturantson the map for you. Click them and check them out. You can click and check out any of the markers on the map. Anywhere on the map that you click more information on the location comes up. When you click on an existing marker it will tell you the name, location, telephone number, how many stars the location has and more. Use the symbol in the top left hand corner to look at the list of tourist attractions, accomdation and resturants in that location. Click on each location. Click the back arrow to go back to list. Click the symbol in the top left hand side corner again to go back to the map.

There are also more options in the top right hand corner the first symbol allows you to share the post and the second allows you to make the map larger on its own page. The plus and minus buttons in the bottom left hand corner allow you to zoom in and out of the map.


World map

Have a look at the world map, our travel team have pin pointed a few holiday and beach spots on the world map for you. Click them and check them out. Also anywhere on the map that you click a marker will be left there for you. Each red marker is a holiday destination and each flag is a beach holiday. When you click on an existing marker it will tell you the location. Use the plus and minus symbols to zoom in and out. Also use the yellow man in the bottom corner, place him on the map where you would like to perhaps go to, once he is placed you can see what is there, in that location.

There are also links at the top on the page to tours around the world, flights and social media pages. Click tours to find out more about the different tours. Click on social media platforms instagram, facebook and twitter to find insporation on where to travel to next.
